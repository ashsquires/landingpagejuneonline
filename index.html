How it works

Collaborative Commerce with Silverwing Price Curves

The key to our infrastructure are Silverwing Price Curves. With a wide range of applications this patent-pending technology allows buyers and suppliers to effortlessly collaborate to unlock new value that was previously unobtainable. How we do this is outlined below:

  • Login

    Login through multiple options, including custom marketplaces, supplier websites, exisiting e-procurement solutions or the Silverwing marketplace

  • Purchase

    Purchase a wide range of products, delivered immediately from the supplier. Purchase volumes are aggregated across all buyers, meaning that when any other organisation purchases the same product, or you decide to buy more, the price decreases even further

  • Earn

    As the price decreases, those who bought at a higher price receive credits for the difference. All credits are securely stored in your Silverwing Wallet

How it works

Invest Your SW Credits

These new SW credits represent new value (Alpha). Invest your credits at your discretion:

  • Future Purchases

    Apply them to future orders, be it to discount everyday purchases or fund a moonshot project that you may not have previously had the budget for

  • Bottom Line

    Apply SW Credits straight to your bottom line by withdrawing them as cash to your bank account
