Our Vision

Silverwing enables an ecosystem where buyers and suppliers collaborate to grow

Suppliers

Suppliers reach existing and new customers earlier in the purchasing cycle, to guide buyers in real-time towards decisions that save money and/or meet the suppliers’ strategic needs.

By sharing some of these cost savings and rewarding buyers for repeat purchases and referrals, suppliers can increase loyalty and convert buyers into a salesforce.

Buyers

Management has peace of mind that they are paying the lowest price and getting the most from their purchasing power, while getting more control over their purchases (what, when and who).

End-users are empowered to buy what they need when they need it, from pre-approved suppliers in a fast and hassle-free way.
